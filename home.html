About APAER

APAER Institute Rusororo is a faith-based educational institution located in Rusororo, Rwanda. We combine strong academic programs with discipline and Christian values to nurture students intellectually, morally, and practically.

Our Academic Levels

Structured learning pathways designed to build knowledge, skills, and career readiness.

O'Level

Strong foundational secondary education focused on core subjects and competency-based learning to prepare students for national examinations.

Level 3

Entry-level technical and vocational training that develops basic practical skills in line with Rwanda Polytechnic standards.

Level 4

Intermediate technical education combining theory and hands-on practice, preparing learners for professional certification and advancement.

Level 5

Advanced vocational and technical training focused on industry-ready skills, leadership, and successful transition to employment or higher education.
