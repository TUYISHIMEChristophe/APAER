About APAER Institute Rusororo

Building academic excellence, discipline, and practical skills.

School Introduction

APAER Institute Rusororo (Association des Parents Adventistes pour l’Éducation) is a faith-based educational institution located in Rusororo Sector, Gasabo District, City of Kigali. The school provides quality education from Nursery to Secondary and Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET), with a strong emphasis on discipline, moral values, and practical skills.

Our History

APAER Institute Rusororo was founded by the Association des Parents Adventistes with the aim of providing accessible and quality education to the community. Since its establishment, the school has grown steadily, expanding its academic and TVET programs to meet national education standards.

Founding Year: Established to serve learners with strong academic foundations, Christian values, and employable skills.

Vision & Mission

Vision:
To be a leading educational institution that produces disciplined, skilled, and morally upright students who contribute positively to national development.

Mission:
To provide quality education and practical training through competent teaching, discipline, and Christian values, preparing learners for further studies, employment, and self-reliance.
