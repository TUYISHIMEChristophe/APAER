Our Programs

APAER Institute Rusororo offers a strong O’Level curriculum from Senior 1 to Senior 3, focusing on core subjects, academic foundations, and Christian values. In addition, our TVET Level IV–V programs provide six practical combinations—Networking, Computer Applications, Tourism, Accounting, Electronic Services, and Masonry—designed to equip students with hands-on skills for employment and self-reliance.
